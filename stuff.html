Fahrenheit 451

Fahrenheit 451 is a dystopian novel set in a world where books are banned and firemen are not the ones who put out fires, but rather the ones who start them. Reading the book in the modern era is really interesting as there are so many devices and ideas talked about that never even existed back in the 1950s when the book was written. One example is the 'Seashell ear-thimbles' which are essentially wireless earbuds enabling "an electronic ocean of sound, of music and talk"

Overall a great read, little hard to understand at times with all the made up words, but still... very cool
